Title: Pick a place to eat for the indecisive lunch goer.

Description: Fredrigo is a DeepDive student and only has an hour for lunch but does not know where to go to eat that is not very far.

Usage Postconditions: Fredrigo is able to find a restaurant to eat at within the alloted time for lunch.
