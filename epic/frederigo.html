Use Case

Title: Pick a place to eat for the indecisive lunch goer.

Description: Frederigo is a DeepDive student and only has an hour for lunch but does not know where to go to eat that is not very far.

Name of the User and their role Frederigo, a professional student, can't decide on where to go eat for his hour lunch, but he enjoys trying new places but doesn't know the area very well.

Usage Preconditions: Frederigo already has an account and is signed in.

Usage Postconditions: Frederigo is able to find a restaurant to eat at within the alloted time for lunch.

Interaction Flow.

  • Frederigo searches for whatsforlunch.com in the search engine.
  • The site displays the main page that includes a map and a restaurant randomizer. It also displays a create account/login on the top of the site.
  • Frederigo is already signed in and clicks the randomizer so he doesnt have to decide what to eat.
  • The site displays a random restaurant within the mile radius of the CNM Stemulus center, and shows it on the map.
  • Frederigo goes to the restaurant displayed and enjoys the food. He liked it so much he wants to add it to his favorites tab.
  • The site diplays a favorite button at the bottom of the restaurant description.
  • Frederigo clicks the favorites button, It then shows the restaurants name in his accounts favorite tab.
