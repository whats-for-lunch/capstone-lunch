Use Case

Title: Pick a place to eat for the indecisive lunch goer.

Description: Frederigo is a DeepDive student and only has an hour for lunch but does not know where to go to eat that is not very far.

Name of the User and their role Frederigo, a professional student, can't decide on where to go eat for his hour lunch, but he enjoys trying new places but doesn't know the area very well.

Usage Preconditions: Frederigo already has an account and is signed in.

Usage Postconditions: Frederigo is able to find a restaurant to eat at within the alloted time for lunch.

Interaction Flow.

  • Frederigo searches for whatsforlunch.com in the search engine.
  • The site displays the main page that includes a map and a restaurant randomizer.
